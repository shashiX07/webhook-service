Info & Usage

  • Data Retention: Webhook endpoints and requests are kept for 1 hour after their last use. Unused endpoints are auto-deleted.
  • Saving: Endpoints are saved in your browser (localStorage) and in the database for persistence across sessions and server restarts.
  • Refresh: The request list auto-refreshes every 2 seconds. You can also use the Refresh button to update manually.
  • Clear: Use the Clear All button to delete all requests for your endpoint.
Tip: You can generate a new endpoint anytime. All data is private to your browser and your endpoint.
